# Evri (via MHI) — Rates & Surcharges

**Status:** Current carrier. Agreed rate via MHI direct contract.
**Effective date:** 12 March 2026
**Contract route:** Via MHI (Master Handling Integrator) — direct contract, not retail Evri rates.
**Delivery model:** Door-to-door. UK excluding Highlands & Islands (separate rate applies).
**Size limits:** Max 120cm length / 245cm length + girth.
**Fuel:** Included in all quoted rates — no separate fuel surcharge.

## Surcharges

| Surcharge | Amount | Trigger |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Collection surcharge | £50.00 per collection | Volume falls below 140 parcels per pick-up |
| Volumetric | £0.60 per parcel | Parcel volume exceeds 40 litres |
| Relabelling | £0.96 per label | Address change or weight band change |
| Return to sender | Same as outbound rate | Undeliverable parcels returned |
| Fuel | Included | Already in all quoted rates above |

---

## Surcharge-Inclusive Rates (for toggle)

Fuel is already included. The standard per-parcel surcharges are conditional (volumetric, relabelling) so base rates are effectively the with-surcharge rates for typical shipments. The collection surcharge of £50 per collection is a threshold-triggered event-level charge, not a per-parcel add-on.

For the toggle, no adjustment is needed to the base rates — fuel is already baked in.
