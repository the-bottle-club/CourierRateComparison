# InPost — Rates & Surcharges

**Status:** Agreed rates. 0.24p saving negotiated on final rate.
**Delivery model:** Out-of-Home (OOH) — locker and parcel shop delivery. Customers must collect. Not door-to-door.
**Injection point:** Waltham Cross EN3 7XQ — 22 miles / ~35 mins from IG11 7TS. Inject by 20:00 Mon–Fri.
**Network:** 15,000+ lockers and parcel shops across the UK.
**Extended regions:** Not available at quoted rates.

---

## Weight Bands

InPost uses 3 size bands (not the same as the 5-band breakdown used by other carriers):

| Band | Weight |
|------|--------|
| Small | 0–3kg |
| Medium | 3–15kg |
| Large | 15–30kg |
